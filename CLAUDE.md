# The Brand - Script - Website

Company website for "The Brand - Script -" SRL, a social media marketing agency based in Bucharest, Romania. Owner: Axel (rotaru_mihai00@yahoo.com).

## Stack
Single page HTML/CSS/JS — no frameworks, no build tools.

## Design
- Minimalistic, dark theme
- Colors: black (#0a0a0a), gold (#C9A84C), white
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)

## Sections

2. Plans — Plan A (€500/mo, 10 posts), Plan B (€2500/mo, 200 posts + Google Ads)
3. About — company story and services
4. Contact — email, phone, location
